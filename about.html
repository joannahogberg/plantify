WE ARE PLANTIFY

PLANTIFY started in Stockholm in 2016 by Steffe Stickling and Petronella, just Petronella.

We want to create new enhanced environments, with water and plants to promote inspiration and enhance the vitality of people in different workplaces. Our employees have been selected carefully and they all have over 10 years of extensive experience in the industry in various management positions, selling roles, technical roles and administrative roles.

PLANTIFY'S MISSION

Plantify revolutionizes the way you think about plants in an office environment. Plantify isn't just a a delivery service for plants, it's a delivery service for new ideas. Plantify makes it easy to keep your workplace fresh and oxygenated. A workplace that is a alive create ideas that live on.
