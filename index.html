WELCOME TO PLANTIFY

Do you dream of feel close to nature at your job? PLANTIFY offers a unique plant decoration suitable for offices and public spaces.

Decorating with plants is a simple way to create an inspiring working environment for all employees. PLANTIFY creates natural plant interiors that makes both visitors and employees feeling welcome in all public places.

Plants have a recognized positive effect on people's well-being and performance. A green interior design not only improves the oxygenation of your office, but also dampens the noise level in the premises. Simply a great way to create a healthier, calmer and more stimulating office environment.

PLANTIFY helps you with everything from single flowers to major events and installations. Our goal is always to create a pleasant interior to your liking.

We are constantly developing our products to meet our customers' desire for quality and design. Therefore, we can always offer a wide range of solutions for office interiors with plants in close cooperation with our partners in the industry.

When you hire us, you always get access to the latest in the market for interior landscaping.
